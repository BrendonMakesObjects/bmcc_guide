Fiterman Cafe

The Cafe of the Fiterman building is located on the first floor you can grab a coffee there and relax.

Main Building: Cafeteria & Outside Sitting Area

The cafeteria is located on the second floor, There are several places where you can sit and eat also use computer if you get bored. Other than that, if you'd like to go outside and get some fresh air there is a door located to the right of the SGA office that leads to the sitting area which is open to all students.

Main Building: Outside 3rd Floor

On the third floor directly across the bursars office is an area where anyone can sit in the sun(if it's out) and relax.
