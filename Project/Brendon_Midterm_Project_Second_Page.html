Fiterman Building Study Room's

7th-9th & 5th Floors

On the fifth floor in room 511 is the study room which is not open all the time but when it is you should go there and see how much work you can get done it's quieter than most other rooms. From the 7th-9th Floor are study rooms numbered 713,813, & 913, Great place to study.

Specially Reserved rooms

Main Building Study Area

On the 4th Floor there is the library which is obviously a great place to study, there are several places to sit, not to mention that you can get a seat in one of the many cubicle's in the library. Adjacent to the library entrance is the Quiet Study Room where anyone can go to get a study session started.
On the 5th Floor, there are directly above the library is the Tutoring section this is where BMCC's professional tutor's are located, there are tutor's for every subject.

Computer Labs Around Campus

BMCC has an array of computer labs at student disposal, the computer labs in the main building are located in rooms S-115, S510 . Lastly in the Fiterman Building the computer labs are located in room LL03 & LL04
